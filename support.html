How do I scan a menu with Happy Munch?
Open the Happy Munch app and tap the camera button. Point your phone at any restaurant menu and take a photo. Our AI will instantly analyze the menu and highlight the best dishes for your dietary needs. Works with physical menus, digital screens, and even handwritten specials!
What types of menus can Happy Munch scan?
Happy Munch can scan:
  • Physical printed menus
  • Digital menu boards and screens
  • Handwritten specials boards
  • QR code menus on phones
  • Photos of menus from any source
  • Menus in 50+ languages

  • 3 menu scans per day

  • Meal history tracking

Is Happy Munch safe for severe food allergies?
Happy Munch uses advanced AI to detect allergens with 99% accuracy. However, for severe allergies, always verify with restaurant staff. Our app flags potential cross-contamination risks but should not replace direct communication with the restaurant. Read our full allergy safety guide.
Which allergens can Happy Munch detect?
We detect all major allergens including:
  • Gluten and wheat
  • Tree nuts and peanuts
  • Dairy and lactose
  • Soy
  • Shellfish and fish
  • Eggs
  • Sesame
  • And many more...
How does the Waiter Mode work for allergies?
Waiter Mode generates language cards in 50+ languages that clearly communicate your allergies and dietary restrictions to restaurant staff. Simply show your phone to the waiter - no language barriers, no misunderstandings.
