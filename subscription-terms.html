📱 Important Subscription Information

Payment & Billing:

  • Payment will be charged to your Apple ID account at confirmation of purchase
  • Subscription automatically renews unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24-hours before the end of the current period
  • Account will be charged for renewal within 24-hours prior to the end of the current period
  • The renewal charge will be the same as your original subscription price

Free Tier Information

Happy Munch offers a free tier with the following limitations:

  • 3 free meal analyses per month - Perfect for occasional use
  • Monthly limit resets on the same day each month you first signed up
  • Access to basic features and recommendations
  • Upgrade anytime to unlock unlimited analyses

Premium Features

With Happy Munch Premium, you get:

  • Unlimited Meal Analyses: Scan as many menus as you want, whenever you want
  • Complete Meal History: Access your full dining history and track patterns
  • Advanced AI Recommendations: Get personalized suggestions based on your preferences and goals
  • All Dietary Filters: Apply multiple dietary restrictions and preferences simultaneously
  • Priority Support: Get help faster when you need it
  • New Features First: Be the first to access new features as we release them

🔄 Easy Cancellation

How to Cancel Your Subscription:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone or iPad
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap "Subscriptions"
  4. Select "Happy Munch Premium"
  5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"

What happens when you cancel:

  • You'll continue to have Premium access until the end of your current billing period
  • Your subscription won't auto-renew
  • You can resubscribe anytime to regain Premium features
  • Your data and meal history are preserved

Managing Your Subscription

You can manage your subscription directly through your Apple ID:

  • View subscription status: Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions
  • Change subscription tier: Select a different plan in your subscription settings
  • Update payment method: Settings > [Your Name] > Payment & Shipping
  • Turn off auto-renewal: Toggle off in your subscription settings

Refund Policy

All subscription purchases are handled through Apple's App Store. For refund requests:

  • Visit reportaproblem.apple.com
  • Sign in with your Apple ID
  • Find your Happy Munch purchase
  • Select "Report a Problem" and follow the prompts

Apple's refund policies apply to all subscription purchases.

Price Changes

If we change subscription prices:

  • Existing subscribers will be notified at least 30 days in advance
  • You'll have the option to accept the new price or cancel
  • Price increases require your consent through the App Store
  • Your current price is locked in until you accept any increase

Family Sharing

Happy Munch Premium subscriptions support Family Sharing when available:

  • Share your subscription with up to 5 family members
  • Each family member gets their own account and personalized experience
  • Privacy is maintained - meal histories and preferences are not shared
  • Set up through Settings > [Your Name] > Family Sharing

Free Trial Information

When we offer free trials:

  • New users may be eligible for a free trial period
  • Free trial length and availability varies by promotion
  • You won't be charged during the free trial period
  • Cancel anytime during the trial to avoid charges
  • If you don't cancel, you'll be automatically charged when the trial ends

Contact & Support

Have questions about your subscription?

  • Email: support@happy-munch.com
  • In-App: Profile > Settings > Contact Support
  • Response Time: Within 24 hours for Premium subscribers
