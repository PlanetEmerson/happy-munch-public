Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Happy Munch

Is Happy Munch really free?

Yes! You get 3 free scans per day, macro tracking, and language cards. Upgrade to Premium for unlimited scans and advanced features.

How accurate is the allergy detection?

We achieve 99% accuracy in detecting major allergens like gluten, nuts, dairy, soy, and shellfish. However, always verify with restaurant staff for severe allergies.

Does it work offline?

Yes! Previously scanned menus and meals work without internet. Perfect for traveling or areas with poor connectivity.

Can it track my macros?

Absolutely! Happy Munch instantly calculates protein, carbs, fats, and calories for any dish. Perfect for fitness goals and meal planning.

What diets does it support?

All of them! Keto, paleo, vegan, vegetarian, Mediterranean, diabetic-friendly, low-FODMAP, Whole30, low-sodium, high-protein, intermittent fasting, carnivore, halal, kosher, and more. Plus, we've expanded how you tell us about your needs - separate your true allergies from dietary choices and personal preferences for smarter recommendations.

Is my data private?

Yes. We only store your food preferences to give better recommendations. You can delete all data anytime in Settings. We never sell your information.
